Connect the Power (Red Wire)

Connect Arduino Pin 13 to the LED's long leg:

✓ Take a red jumper wire

✓ Connect one end to Arduino Pin 13

✓ Connect the other end to the same row as the LED's long leg

Why Pin 13? Arduino boards have a built-in LED on Pin 13, making it perfect for our first project. We'll control this pin with our code.
Power Note: Your Arduino gets power through the USB cable connected to your computer. This same cable is used to upload code and provides 5V power to run the board.
