Add the Resistor

Now add a resistor to protect the LED from too much current:

✓ Take the 220 ohm resistor (red-red-brown-gold bands)

✓ Push one leg into the same row as the LED's short leg

✓ Push the other leg into an empty row below

Why a resistor? LEDs are sensitive to current. Without a resistor, too much current would flow through and burn out the LED. The 220 ohm resistor limits the current to a safe level.
