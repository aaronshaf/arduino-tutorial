Complete the Circuit (Black Wire)

Finally, connect the ground wire to complete the circuit:

✓ Take a black jumper wire

✓ Connect one end to Arduino GND (ground)

✓ Connect the other end to the same row as the bottom of the resistor

Your circuit is now complete! The current flows from Pin 13 → through the LED → through the resistor → back to ground (GND).
