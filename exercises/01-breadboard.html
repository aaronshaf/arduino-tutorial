How a Breadboard Works

A breadboard lets you connect parts without soldering. Here's how it works:

Simple Rules:

  1. Holes in same row = connected
  2. Different rows = not connected
  3. Center gap = keeps left and right separate

Why This Matters

Understanding how a breadboard works is crucial because:

  • Your LED legs need to be on different sides of the gap
  • Wires in the same row will connect to each other
  • The gap prevents short circuits
Remember: If you put both LED legs on the same side, they won't be connected to anything!
