What is a Breadboard?

A breadboard is like a LEGO board for electronics. You can stick parts in and take them out!

Let's Start Simple

Look at your breadboard. See all those little holes? That's where we put our parts.

The Secret: Rows are Connected!

Here's the magic: holes in the same row share electricity!

But the Gap Keeps Things Apart

The gap in the middle is like a wall. Left side and right side don't touch.

Why This Helps Us

🔌 We use the breadboard to connect our parts

🔗 Parts in the same row share electricity

🚫 The gap keeps things from touching that shouldn't
