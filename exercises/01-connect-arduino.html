First: Connect Your Arduino

Before we start building, let's connect your Arduino to your computer:

✓ Take the USB cable that came with your Arduino kit

✓ Plug the square end (USB-B) into your Arduino

✓ Plug the flat end (USB-A) into your computer

✓ The green PWR (power) LED on your Arduino should light up

What the USB cable does:
  • Provides 5V power to run your Arduino
  • Allows you to upload code from your computer
  • Lets your Arduino send messages back to your computer
Tip: If the power LED doesn't turn on, try a different USB port on your computer. Some USB ports provide more power than others.
